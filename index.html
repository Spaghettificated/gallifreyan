Gallifreyan scribe

Gallifreyan is a language of the Time Lords from the Doctor Who universe
Inspired by their circular writings in a show fans created their own writing systems imitating it
This little tool based on sherman Gallifryan
let's you write your own Gallifreyan words while preserving freedom of expression this writing system provides
